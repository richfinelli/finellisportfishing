About

Finelli Sport Fishing provides charter fishing trips for flounder, seabass, as well as, big game fishing for tuna, shark, swordfish, and marlin. We also specialize in sunset cruises and corporate entertainment. Contact Capt. Mark Finelli at mark_finelli@hotmail.com or 609-332-8369. Capt. Mark is a Coast Guard licensed captain. Our primary boat, The Nicole Kathleen, is a 2005 38' Henriques Flybridge equipped with state of the art electronics and safety equipment.
