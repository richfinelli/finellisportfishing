About

Finelli Sport Fishing provides charter fishing trips for flounder, seabass, as well as, big game fishing for tuna, shark, swordfish, and marlin. We also specialize in sunset cruises and corporate entertainment. Contact Capt. Mark Sr. with questions while the site gets up and running.
